Watching in the Night

With each of our kids, my wife has experienced Braxton-Hicks or false labor contractions in pregnancy.  These contractions increase gradually, but inconsistently, in that season of end-times expectancy, near 40 weeks of days.  The truth is, it's hard to distinguish false labor from labor until the baby begins to appear.  If you haven't had kids, this won't make sense.  The uncertainly of labor was especially apparent in our fourth.  We suspected Braxton-Hicks until the end, where we found out false labor had been final labor!

Even when you see it coming, it's an unanticipated instant in time when it comes.  It was an unanticipated instant when Joseph had to find shelter for Mary, although they had seen it coming for some time.

Luke 2:7

And she brought forth her firstborn son, and wrapped him in swaddling clothes, and laid him in a manger; because there was no room for them in the inn.

Similar to His First Coming, Jesus has revealed that His Second Coming will be preceded by labor or travail on a global scale.

Matthew 24:8

All these are the beginning of sorrows [travail].

1 Thessalonians 5:3

For when they say, “Peace and safety [security]!” then sudden destruction comes upon them, as labor pains upon a pregnant woman. And they shall not escape.

Like it was at His first appearing, the instant of His second appearing will be proclaimed by an angel.

Luke 2:8-10

Now there were in the same country shepherds living out in the fields, keeping watch over their flock by night. And behold, an angel of the Lord stood before them, and the glory of the Lord shone around them, and they were greatly afraid. Then the angel said to them, "Do not be afraid, for behold, I bring you good tidings of great joy which will be to all people."

1 Thessalonians 4:16

For the Lord Himself will descend from heaven with a shout, with the voice of an archangel, and with the trumpet of God. And the dead in Christ will rise first.

40 Weeks

In pregnancy, signs of labor increase as 40 weeks of days approach, and then the child appears.  In the timeline of history (or His-Story), we are approaching 40 weeks of seven years, or Sabbatical Years, since the Sabbatical Year when Jesus was crucified, rose again, and ascended into Heaven.  All around us are signs of labor.

40 * (7 * 7 + 1) = 2000

If Jesus ascended in 30 AD, we are nearing 7 years from the end of 40 Sabbatical weeks.
