📖  Sending Bibles & resources to brothers and sisters around the globe

About

I'm here in northern Alabama, enabling collection and transport of Bibles and study materials that are sent to our brothers and sisters on other continents.

Matt2446.us is just one of many small organizations which are able to receive and transport materials to collection hubs. Other organizations are able to provide bulk shipments overseas and have connections with local Christians and pastors in various places.

There is demand all around the globe for materials in common languages, especially English.

Additionally, the U.S. is a great place to obtain God's Word in languages of places where Bibles are difficult to obtain or restricted. Often it is possible to ship Bibles from the U.S. to those places.



📚 

How can I help?

Do you have extra Bibles or Bible study materials sitting on a shelf?

Local pastors and evangelists in places all around the world are requesting resources in English, as well as in their native languages.

ⓘ Print a "Wanted" poster!

Here in the U.S. we can buy cheap interlinear Bibles and Bibles in other languages with one tap on a smartphone.

Will you leave all those resources sitting in an Amazon distribution center?

Contact

To coordinate a pick-up or to obtain more information, please email:

info@matt2446.us

If you are involved with a church, we would encourage you to think about starting a drop box to collect materials to donate.

We are looking for churches that might be willing to act as collection points. Major distribution hubs exist across the U.S. and are operated by various independent organazations. We can facilitate transportation between collection points and major hubs.

If you would like to learn more about the need for Bibles and resources around the globe, as well as other organizations involved in this effort, please reach out. Additional information can be found at bf.org/bibledrive
