Bibles in Alabama

I'm located in the Huntsville, Alabama area and can assist with the collection of Bibles, study resources, or BibleSD cards, to send to our brothers and sisters around the globe.

Pastors and evangelists worldwide continue to request resources in English, as well as in their native languages.

In addition to English, the U.S. is a great place to obtain God's Word in languages of places where Bibles are difficult to obtain or restricted. Often it's possible to ship Bibles from the U.S. to those places.

Matt2446.us is joining a network of contacts to facilitate movement of Bibles and materials to collection hubs. Various organizations provide bulk shipments overseas and have connections with local Christians and pastors.
