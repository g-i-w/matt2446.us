📖  Sending Bibles & resources to brothers and sisters around the globe

North Alabama

Hi! We're based in north-eastern Alabama and are collecting used Bibles and study materials to send to brother's and sisters on other continents. We're one of many small organizations in the U.S. which are able to receive and transport materials to collection hubs.

Other organizations are able to provide bulk shipment overseas and have connections with local Christians and pastors in various places.

Even though most Bibles and study materials in the U.S. are in English, there is still considerable demand in non-English speaking regions for materials in common languages, especially English.

The U.S. is still a great place to obtain God's Word in many languages of regions where Bibles are difficult to obtain or are restricted. Often you can ship Bibles from the U.S. to those regions.



📚  How can I help?

Do you have extra Bibles or Bible study materials sitting on a shelf?

Local pastors and evangelists in places all around the world are requesting resources in English, as well as in their native languages.

Here in the U.S. we can also buy cheap interlinear Bibles and Bibles in other languages with one tap on a smartphone.

Will you leave all those resources sitting in an Amazon distribution center?



ⓘ   Contact

To coordinate a pick-up or to obtain more information, please email:

info@matt2446.us

If you are involved with a church, we would encourage you to think about starting a drop box to collect materials to donate.

We are looking for churches in the northern Alabama area which are willing to act as local collection points. Major distribution hubs exist across the U.S., operated by multiple independent organazations. We can facilitate transport between collection points and major hubs.

If you would like to learn more about the need for Bibles and resources around the globe, as well as other organizations involved in this effort, feel free to reach out. Additional information about the need can be found at bf.org/bibledrive
